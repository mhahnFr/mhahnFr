```Swift
let i = data[0] & 0xff << 24
      | data[1] & 0xff << 16
      | data[2] & 0xff <<  8
      | data[3] & 0xff <<  0
```
<details><summary><b>Correct solution</b> <i>(Click to expand)</i></summary>

```Swift
let i = Int(data[0]) & 0xff << 24
      | Int(data[1]) & 0xff << 16
      | Int(data[2]) & 0xff <<  8
      | Int(data[3]) & 0xff <<  0
```
</details>
